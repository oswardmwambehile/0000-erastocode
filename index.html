KKT HOSPITAL, MTEMA BEACH

KKT Hospital at Mtema Beach, Kyela, Mbeya, is a well-known healthcare facility in the Southern Highlands of Tanzania. Our mission is to provide quality health care services to all patients, regardless of faith or socio-economic status.

Our team consists of dedicated medical professionals including specialists, medical doctors, assistant medical officers, intern doctors, and nurses. Patients come from regions as far as Mozambique, Dar es Salaam, and Zanzibar. The hospital has inpatient facilities with multiple wards and provides both outpatient and inpatient services annually.

KKT Hospital offers basic and specialized services using modern medical equipment. We have a well-equipped laboratory, ICU, NICU, and dialysis units. Advanced equipment like CT-Scan, digital x-ray, laparoscopy, video-endoscopy, and high-end ultrasound machines are available to ensure the best care for patients. Our staff is highly trained and motivated to provide optimal healthcare services.
